Boon

Artists, meet venues. Venues, meet artists.

Who We Are

Boon is a music connection platform that efficiently allows artists and venues to connect and communicate directly, effectively eliminating third party promoters giving artists and venues alike autonomy over their next bookings.

Who are our users?

Our platform services both artists looking for a place to play and venues looking for new performers; labels and A&R teams can also use this platform to track new artists and see their performance in the live music setting

How does it work?

Sign up, create a profile, fill out your preferences, and we will launch you into a whole new network of up and coming artists and venues

Why Boon?

Getting started in the music industry, either as an artist or as a venue can be a daunting task... there is no blueprint as to how to get yourself out there, and the blueprint that exists involves a third party promoter that rarely benefits the artist. As a venue, there is no easy way to gain access to artists conveniently and quickly. Boon works to solve all of these issues by simply providing connections and a place to get started.

Boon provides the solution for small artists and venues for getting started in the industry, but we also provide this solution in a way that respects the relationship between artist and venue. As a community founded by artists looking for venues, we understand the flaws of the current climate and are looking to make a change in the way that networking is done in the industry for the better.

Through Boon, an artist will be able to expand their music network across all cities, giving them the chances that they deserve or otherwise may not be able have.
